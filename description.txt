"Mootisma's Collection" is a non-profit, non-commercial Terraria Mod that I work on by myself for fun.

This mod contains art and audio that does not belong to me; I didn't make these. Sources are given in the mod and in text outside of the in-game mod browser.
This mod contains content based on the game Arknights, Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, and Zenless Zone Zero.

If you have suggestions, feedback, or bug reports, you can comment on the steam post.
